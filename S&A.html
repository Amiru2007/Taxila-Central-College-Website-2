Taxila Astronomical Association

In 1997 , the Astronomy Society of our college was formed based on the idea of crew of students, chiefly the student Mohan Chaminda Mallawarachchi. We are working hard to increase the popularity of Astronomy in Sri Lanka.

Taxila Senior Science Society

Senior Science Society has been doing a remarkable job to uplift the name of our school to the globe. We inculcate attitudes, update knowledge, uplift the skills and pave the way to students. Except our society there are over 40 other societies in our school to improve to make their life more successful. Science Section, one of the leading Sections, which represents the school, has been doing a tremendous work to uplift the standard of the Science Students.
